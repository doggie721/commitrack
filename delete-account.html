🧾 Delete Your Account – CommiTrack

At CommiTrack, we value your privacy and give you full control over your personal data.

🔐 How to request account deletion

If you would like to delete your CommiTrack account and associated data, please send an email to:

📧 support@commitrack.com

With the subject line: Delete My Account

Please include the email address you used to register in the app.

  • We will verify your identity
  • We will delete your account and all associated data
  • The process will be completed within 7 business days
  • You will receive a confirmation email when it's done

📦 What data will be deleted?

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if provided)
  • All saved commissions and product data

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
